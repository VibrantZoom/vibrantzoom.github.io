Who Is This Website For?

Animals

Humans

Humans use chemistry to accomplish monumental feats, like landing on the moon, and equally important ones, like figuring out how many hot sauce drops will set their tacos on fire but not their mouths.

What Are Chemical Compounds?

Chemical compounds are substances that are made up of atoms from more than 1 chemical element. Chemical compounds are held together by chemical bonds. Here are the 4 main types of chemical compounds:

Molecular compounds
Held together via covalent bonds. (Atoms share electrons)
Ionic compounds
Held together via ionic bonds. (Nonmetals take electrons from metals)
Intermetallic compounds
Held together via metallic bonds
Coordination complexes
Held together by coordinate covalent bonds, has a central aton/ion which is usually metallic.

Chemical compounds can be refered to by their chemical formula. This uses symbols and subscripts to indicate the type and number of atoms involved.

Common chemical compounds will be in the compound list page, while more interesting chemical compounds will have their own page.
